Pensions Enquiry Service

Use this service to:

  • find information regarding NHS pensions
  • submit a enquiry form to NHS pensions

Before you start

You can not use this service if you are not a NHS member

This service only covers enquires on the following areas:

  • retirement pensions
  • estimates of pensions
  • transfers of pensions
  • pensions on divorce and dissolution
  • annual allowance
  • refund of pension benefits
  • bereavement
  • additional benefit
  • nominations, allocations and beneficiaries
